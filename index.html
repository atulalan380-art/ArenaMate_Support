Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How do I create a new match or session?

Open the app and tap on the New Match or similar button, then choose your game type, number of players/teams, and basic settings. Confirm to start tracking the match.

Why are some options disabled in my version?

Certain advanced features might be rolled out gradually or unlocked in newer updates. Please make sure you are using the latest version of ArenaMate from your app store.

The app is not saving my latest changes. What can I do?

First, ensure you have a stable internet connection if your data is synced online. If the problem continues, try restarting the app and your device. If it still persists, contact support with steps to reproduce the issue.

Will my match data be lost if I reinstall the app?

This depends on your platform and whether cloud backup/sync is enabled. We recommend taking a backup (if available in your version) before reinstalling. You can also send a support request if you are unsure.

Troubleshooting

Before contacting support, try these quick checks.

App Not Opening or Crashing

  • Restart your device and try again.
  • Ensure you are on the latest version of the OS.
  • Update ArenaMate to the latest version from your app store.
  • If the issue persists, capture a screenshot or screen recording and send it to support.

Score or Player Data Missing

  • Check if you are viewing the correct match or session.
  • Verify that the app has completed any ongoing sync.
  • Avoid force-closing the app while saving data.

Performance Issues

  • Close other heavy apps running in the background.
  • Ensure you have enough free storage space on your device.
  • Disable battery saver modes that may restrict app performance.

Privacy & Data

How ArenaMate handles your data.

Data We Access

ArenaMate primarily stores match configuration, basic stats, and settings needed to provide the core app functionality. Exact data stored may vary by version and platform.

Device Permissions

Depending on your platform and usage, ArenaMate may request permissions such as storage (to save data locally) or network access (to sync or check for updates).

Third‑Party Services

Some versions of the app may use third‑party services (for analytics, crash reports, or cloud sync). These are used only to improve stability and the user experience.

Your Control

  • You can clear local app data from your device settings.
  • You may revoke permissions such as storage or network access from OS settings (some app features may stop working).
  • You can contact support at any time for clarification about stored data.
